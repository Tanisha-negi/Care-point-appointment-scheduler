What is this doctor appointment scheduler and how does it work? +

Our doctor appointment scheduler is an online platform that allows patients to easily book, reschedule, or cancel appointments with healthcare professionals. Instead of calling the clinic or waiting in line, patients can search for doctors by specialty, location, or availability and confirm appointments within a few clicks. Once a booking is made, both the patient and doctor receive confirmation and reminders to reduce the chances of missed appointments.

Do I need to create an account to book an appointment? +

Yes, patients are required to create an account before booking an appointment. This ensures that your medical history, past appointments, and future bookings are safely stored in one place. Having an account also allows you to manage multiple appointments, receive reminders, and access secure medical notes from your doctor. Signing up is free and only requires basic details like your name, email, and phone number.

Can I book appointments for family members through my account?? +

Absolutely. Our system supports “Family Profiles,” which means you can manage appointments not only for yourself but also for your children, parents, or other dependents. Each profile can be linked under one account, so you don’t need to create separate logins. This makes it convenient to schedule multiple visits without confusion.

How do I search for the right doctor? +

You can search for doctors using filters such as specialty (e.g., cardiologist, dentist, dermatologist), location, consultation type (online or in-clinic), and availability. Each doctor’s profile contains detailed information including qualifications, years of experience, clinic address, and patient reviews. This helps you make an informed decision before confirming an appointment.

Are the doctors listed verified and qualified? +

Yes, every doctor listed on our platform goes through a strict verification process. We verify their medical license, registration with relevant medical councils, and credentials before they are allowed to offer services on our platform. Patient safety and trust are our top priorities, so you can be confident that all doctors are genuine healthcare professionals.

What types of appointments can I book? +

You can book both in-clinic and online (video/phone) consultations depending on the doctor’s availability. Online consultations are especially useful for follow-ups, minor health concerns, or when traveling. In-clinic visits are recommended for physical examinations or treatments that cannot be done virtually. Each doctor clearly mentions which types of appointments they offer.

Will I receive reminders for my appointments? +

Yes, you will automatically receive reminders via email, SMS, or push notifications (if you enable them). Reminders are usually sent 24 hours before and again 1–2 hours before the scheduled appointment. This ensures you don’t miss your consultation. For online consultations, you’ll also receive the video call link in your reminder.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment after booking? +

Yes, our system is flexible and allows you to cancel or reschedule appointments easily. However, the exact policy depends on the doctor’s clinic. Some doctors allow free cancellations up to 24 hours before the appointment, while others may have a cancellation fee. All policies are clearly displayed before you confirm your booking so there are no surprises later.

Is there a fee for using this scheduler? +

Creating an account and browsing doctors is completely free. The only cost involved is the consultation fee charged by the doctor, which you will see clearly before confirming your appointment. Some clinics may also offer packages, health plans, or discounts for online payments. Our platform itself does not charge extra service fees to patients for booking appointments.

How secure is my personal and medical information? +

We use advanced encryption and secure servers to store all your personal and medical information. Only you and your chosen doctor can access your medical records, and we never share your data with third parties without your consent. Our platform is designed to comply with healthcare privacy standards such as HIPAA (if applicable) to ensure maximum confidentiality.

How do I create an account on the platform? +

To create an account, click on the “Sign Up” button on the homepage. You’ll be asked to provide your basic details such as name, email, phone number, and password. After filling in the form, you’ll receive a verification email or SMS to confirm your identity. Once verified, your account will be active, and you can immediately start booking appointments.

Why do I need to verify my email or phone number? +

Email or phone verification ensures that your account is secure and that you can receive important updates about your appointments. Verification also prevents fake accounts from being created, which helps keep the platform safe for both doctors and patients. Without verification, you may not be able to book or manage appointments.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it? +

If you forget your password, simply click on the “Forgot Password” link on the login page. You’ll be asked to enter your registered email or phone number. A One-Time Password (OTP) or reset link will be sent to you. Follow the instructions to set a new password and regain access to your account. Make sure to choose a strong password for better security.

Can I change my registered email address or phone number? +

Yes, you can update your email or phone number by going to the “Account Settings” section. For security reasons, you may be asked to re-enter your password or confirm the new contact details with an OTP. Once updated, all future communications will be sent to your new email or phone number.

How do I update my personal details such as name, age, or address? +

You can easily edit your profile by visiting the “Profile Settings” section in your account. There, you can update details like your full name, date of birth, gender, and address. Keeping your profile updated ensures that doctors have accurate information during consultations and helps avoid confusion in your medical records.

Can I create multiple accounts with the same email or phone number? +

No, each email address and phone number can only be linked to one account. This policy helps maintain the integrity and security of patient data. If you want to manage appointments for multiple family members, you can use the “Family Profile” feature instead of creating multiple accounts.

What should I do if I suspect someone else is using my account? +

If you believe your account has been compromised, immediately change your password from the “Security Settings” section. If you cannot log in, use the password reset option. Additionally, contact our support team to secure your account and investigate any unauthorized activity. We also recommend enabling two-factor authentication (if available) for extra protection.

Is my account information shared with doctors or third parties? +

Your personal information is kept private and secure. Only the doctors you book appointments with can access relevant details, such as your medical history, to provide accurate care. We do not share your data with advertisers or third-party companies without your explicit consent. Our platform is designed to comply with privacy regulations to keep your information safe.

Can I delete my account if I no longer wish to use the service? +

Yes, you can permanently delete your account from the “Account Settings” section. Deleting your account will remove all personal data, booking history, and linked profiles from our system. However, some medical records may be retained as required by law. Once deleted, your account cannot be restored, and you’ll need to sign up again if you wish to use the service in the future.

How do I keep my account secure? +

To keep your account secure, always use a strong password that includes letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid sharing your login details with anyone, and always log out if you’re using a public device. We also recommend enabling security features like OTP verification for sensitive actions (password reset, profile changes) to protect your data.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept multiple payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. Some clinics may also allow cash payments at the time of your in-clinic visit. If you are booking an online consultation, digital payment is required to confirm your appointment.

Do I need to pay before booking an appointment? +

In most cases, yes. Pre-payment secures your slot with the doctor and prevents last-minute cancellations. However, some doctors allow “Pay at Clinic” for in-person visits. This option will be clearly shown before you confirm your appointment so you can choose the method that suits you.

Are there any hidden fees or extra charges? +

No, we are transparent about all charges. The amount displayed on the booking page includes the doctor’s consultation fee and any applicable service charges. There are no hidden fees. If a doctor charges additional fees for procedures or tests during an in-clinic visit, you will be informed directly at the clinic.

Will I get an invoice or receipt after payment? +

Yes, once your payment is completed, a digital receipt is automatically generated and sent to your registered email. You can also download invoices from the “Billing” section in your account. Receipts contain details such as doctor’s name, consultation type, amount paid, payment method, and transaction ID.

Can I cancel my appointment and get a refund? +

Yes, you can cancel your appointment and request a refund if it falls within the doctor’s cancellation policy. Most doctors allow free cancellations up to 24 hours before the appointment. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank or wallet provider.

What happens if the doctor cancels my appointment? +

If the doctor cancels, you will receive a full refund without any deductions. In addition, you may be given the option to reschedule with the same doctor or choose another doctor without paying again. We ensure that patients are not inconvenienced due to doctor-side cancellations.

Can I reschedule an appointment without paying again? +

Yes, rescheduling is usually free as long as you follow the clinic’s policy. If you reschedule within the allowed time frame (e.g., 24 hours before the original slot), your payment will be carried over to the new appointment. If you miss this window, you may have to rebook and pay again.

How secure are my payment details? +

We use industry-standard encryption and payment gateways to process all transactions. Your card or bank details are never stored on our servers. Transactions comply with PCI-DSS security standards to ensure maximum protection of sensitive financial information.

Do you support health insurance or corporate health plans? +

Some doctors and clinics on our platform accept insurance claims or are tied up with corporate health plans. If eligible, you can select the “Pay with Insurance” option at checkout. You may need to upload your insurance details and carry your card during the visit. Availability of insurance support depends on the doctor’s clinic.

What should I do if my payment fails but money is deducted from my account? +

If a payment fails but your bank account shows a deduction, don’t worry. Such transactions are automatically reversed within 5–7 working days by your bank. If you don’t receive a refund within that period, you can contact our support team with your transaction ID, and we’ll coordinate with the payment gateway to resolve the issue quickly.

How do I book an appointment with a doctor? +

To book an appointment, log in to your account and use the search bar to find a doctor by name, specialty, or location. Once you select a doctor, choose your preferred time slot from their available schedule and confirm by making the required payment (if applicable). You’ll receive a confirmation notification and email instantly.

Can I choose between online and in-clinic appointments? +

Yes, most doctors on the platform provide both online consultations and in-clinic visits. When booking, you’ll see two options: “Video Consultation” and “In-Clinic Visit.” Simply select the one that suits you best. If you choose online, you’ll receive a secure video link; if you choose in-clinic, your appointment details will include the clinic’s address.

What information do I need to provide while booking? +

While booking, you’ll need to provide your name, age, gender, contact details, and any relevant medical history. This helps the doctor prepare for your consultation. For online consultations, ensure that your device has a working camera, microphone, and a stable internet connection.

How do I reschedule an appointment? +

You can reschedule an appointment by going to the “My Appointments” section in your account. Choose the appointment you want to change, then select a new date and time from the doctor’s available slots. If rescheduling is within the allowed time frame (usually 24 hours before the consultation), no extra charge will apply.

Can I cancel my appointment? +

Yes, you can cancel appointments as long as it falls within the doctor’s cancellation policy. Simply go to “My Appointments,” select the booking, and click “Cancel.” You’ll be informed about the refund eligibility (if any) before confirming the cancellation. Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 working days.

What happens if I miss my appointment? +

If you miss your appointment without canceling in advance, the consultation fee may not be refunded. However, you can still rebook with the same doctor if slots are available. Some doctors may offer partial refunds or free rescheduling in exceptional cases, but this depends on their policy.

How will I be reminded of my upcoming appointment? +

You’ll receive reminders via SMS, email, and app notifications (if enabled) 24 hours before and again 1 hour before your scheduled appointment. For online consultations, you’ll also receive a direct link to join the video call. These reminders help ensure you don’t miss your consultation.

What should I do if the doctor is late or unavailable? +

If the doctor is delayed, you’ll be notified of the revised time. If the doctor becomes unavailable, your appointment will be automatically rescheduled or refunded. You’ll also have the option to book with another doctor at no extra cost if you prefer.

Can I book appointments for my family members? +

Yes, you can book appointments for family members by adding them under the “Family Profile” section of your account. While booking, simply select which family member the appointment is for. This feature is especially useful for parents booking for children or caregivers booking for elderly family members.

How many appointments can I book at once? +

There’s no strict limit on the number of appointments you can book. You can schedule multiple appointments for yourself and your family members as long as they don’t overlap. However, each appointment needs to be confirmed individually to avoid confusion and ensure accuracy in the doctor’s schedule.
